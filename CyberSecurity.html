What is Cyber Security?

Cyber security is like a lock for your computer and personal information. It helps keep the bad guys (hackers) out and makes sure your belongings are safe. Just like how you lock your house to keep people from coming inside, cyber security locks your digital world to protect your data.

Why is Cyber Security Important?

Cyber security is important because it helps protect your personal information from people who might try to steal it. For example, hackers could steal your passwords or pretend to be you online. It also helps protect your social media accounts and keeps your data safe from bad people who want to cause harm.

The Importance of Strong Passwords

A strong password is like a super strong lock on your locker. If you use simple passwords like “1234” or “password,” bad guys can easily guess them. A strong password has a mix of letters, numbers, and special characters like “!,” “#,” or “@.” You should also use a password manager to keep track of all your strong passwords.

How to Stay Safe on Social Media

When using social media, it's important to use privacy settings so that only your friends can see your posts, pictures, and personal information. You should always be respectful of others and report any inappropriate behavior. Don’t accept friend requests from people you don’t know in real life.
